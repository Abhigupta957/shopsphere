FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take? ▼
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. Free shipping on all orders over $50.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Initiate a return from your account or contact our support team.
How do I track my order? ▼
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. Use it on our website or the carrier's site to get real-time updates.
Are my payment details secure? ▼
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We never store your full card details — everything is processed through certified payment gateways.
Can I cancel or modify my order? ▼
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, they enter processing. Contact support immediately if you need to make changes.
